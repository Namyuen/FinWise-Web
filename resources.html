Resources

Handpicked tools, websites, books, and government programs to help you go deeper on your financial journey.

Official Government Resources

MyMoney.gov

The U.S. government's free financial education portal — covers savings, credit, home buying, and retirement.

Free · Official

CFPB — Money as You Grow

The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau's education resources, including tools for students and families.

Free · Official

Investor.gov (SEC)

The SEC's investor education site — includes compound interest calculators and scam awareness tools.

Free · Official

IRS — Students & Youth

Tax education resources specifically for students filing for the first time, including free filing tools.

Free · Official

Free curriculum and resources for students and teachers covering all aspects of personal finance.

Free · Curriculum

Free · Reference

Recommended Books

The Total Money Makeover

Dave Ramsey

A practical, no-nonsense approach to eliminating debt and building wealth through the famous "baby steps" framework.

Budgeting · Debt

I Will Teach You to Be Rich

Ramit Sethi

Written specifically for 20-somethings — covers automating your finances, credit cards, investing, and negotiating your salary.

Automating · Investing

The Psychology of Money

Morgan Housel

18 short stories about how people think about money — and why behavior matters more than intelligence when it comes to wealth.

Mindset · Investing

Rich Dad Poor Dad

Robert Kiyosaki

A classic that challenges conventional thinking about money, assets, liabilities, and the path to financial independence.

Mindset · Assets
