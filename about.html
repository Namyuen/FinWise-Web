About FinWise

We believe every student deserves access to quality financial education — regardless of where they go to school or how much money their family has.

Our Mission

FinWise was created to bridge the financial literacy gap affecting millions of students. Most schools don't teach personal finance — we do.

Our platform delivers clear, jargon-free financial education through interactive lessons, practical calculators, and comprehensive quizzes that reinforce real-world skills.

The Problem

Studies show that only 23% of young adults understand how interest rates work. Credit card debt, student loan crises, and financial scams disproportionately target people who were never taught the basics.

FinWise exists to change that — one lesson at a time.

What We Offer

  • 📚 5 comprehensive learning modules with 17 lessons
  • 🧮 3 interactive financial calculators
  • 🧠 A 20-question knowledge quiz
  • 🔗 Curated resources and external reading
  • 100% free, no sign-up required

Who We're For

FinWise is built for high school students navigating their first jobs, bank accounts, and financial decisions. Whether you're opening your first checking account or trying to understand what a Roth IRA is, we meet you where you are.

No prior knowledge required. Just curiosity.
